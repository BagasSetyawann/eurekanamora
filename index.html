About Us

Eureka Namora Research Consultant is a consultancy firm specializing in research, surveys, studies, training and education, and statistical data processing. Established in 2019, we have developed an extensive network and provide research consultancy services across 38 provinces and 514 regencies in Indonesia

Vision

To become the leading research consultancy in Indonesia, committed to providing innovative, accurate, and impactful data-driven solutions that support strategic decision making across various sectors throughout the nation

Mission

  • Deliver high-quality research services that adhere to scientific and ethical standards, ensuring the production of relevant and reliable data for our clients' diverse needs
  • Build strategic partnerships with government, private, and non-governmental organizations across Indonesia to support evidence-based decision-making processes.
  • Leverage cutting-edge technology and methodologies to ensure excellence in every research, survey, and data analysis project we undertake.
  • Actively contribute to the development and implementation of research-based policies that promote sustainable progress for the nation.

Values

Methodology

Client-Centered Approach

We begin by thoroughly understanding our clients' unique objectives and challenges by managing every phase of the project, from preliminary studies and literature reviews to analysis and the dissemination of results through Focus Group Discussions (FGD).

Comprehensive Data Collection

We gather data from diverse sources, employing both primary, secondary, and big data sources with a focus on accuracy and quality. For projects requiring specific information, we collect primary data through well-designed surveys using statistical approaches.

Advanced Analysis

Our team of experts, qualified in Data Science, Market Analysis, Public Policy, Surveying, and Training, employs both quantitative and qualitative techniques to deliver high quality insights for informed decision-making.

Continuos Improvement

We stay at the forefront of industry developments through ongoing training and by involving expert advisors as needed

United Nations Development Programme (2020)

Objective

UNDP has mission in developing list of CPC (Selection of Service and Construction)

Tasks

To determine and analysis the Classification of Product Code related to the Goods and Services (Construction) that will be listed in the International Competitive Bidding Standard.

Results

We successfully classified and analyzed Product Codes for Goods and Services (Construction) in accordance with international standards. This classification was integrated into the International Competitive Bidding Standard, providing UNDP with a solid framework for transparent and efficient procurement.

National Public Procurement Agency (2021)

Objective

LKPP has mission in determining the List of Goods Through the Development of an 8-Digit HS Code Classification Model for Government Procurement within the Framework of the IEU-CEPA

Tasks

To conduct quantitative analysis for compiling the list of goods, down to the HS Code level, as a basis for request/offer. Also, prepare the list of goods as a basis for request and offer in the IEU CEPA negotiations.

Results

We developed an 8-digit HS Code classification model and compiled a detailed list of goods, forming the basis for request and offer negotiations in the I-EU CEPA framework.

National Public Procurement Agency (2021)

Objective

LKPP has mission in drafting Dashboards and Classification of Goods/Services Procurement Types

Tasks

To develop data models and selecting classification models and determine the model to be proposed for dashboard creation.

Results

We released recommendations on the depth of classification used based on the model's accuracy at each depth level.

National Public Procurement Agency (2022)

Objective

Studying on Mapping Demand in Government Procurement in European Union Countries to Prepare the Market Access Schedule for IEU CEPA

Tasks

To craft Classification of goods requested by government procurement in European Union countries, based on the Harmonized Code (HS Code). Also, to conduct In-depth analysis of the relationship between the goods classification in item (i) and the readiness, potential, and competitiveness of national businesses to meet this demand.

Results

We generated List of goods with HS Codes that are potential requests from Indonesia to the European Union in the I-EU CEPA, based on the analysis results
